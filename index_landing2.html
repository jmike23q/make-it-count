Make it Count

We are Make it Count, a start-up with the dream to give exactly what is needed to those who need it. Make it Count is a space where individuals in need can create a wish lists on an e-commerce site. Donors are then able to go on our website and buy products, making every dollar of their donation count.

Our advantage is that we provide a unique donating tool that is personalized for each individual, allowing donors to give exact what is needed to those who need it.
